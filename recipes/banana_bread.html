Banana Bread

Description

Banana Bread is the greatest of breads.

You wouldn't think a banana could make a nice bread seeing as they have no arms or legs but they are quite good at it.

If I could eat banana bread everyday I wouldn't.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Dried Basil
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredients in a bowl
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl, add the eggs and bananas.
  3. Add the wet to the dry.
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in oven till ready.
  5. Enjoy!
