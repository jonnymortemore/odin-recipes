Chicken Katsu

Description

First developed by cats in the 16th century, chicken katsu is super yummy.

Somehow bread is all crumbled up and put on top but it all works out.

Serve with rice cause rice is nice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasoning
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Sushi Rice
  • Katsu sauce

Steps

  1. Whisk the egg in a bowl
  2. add flour and bread crumbs to plates
  3. Add the chicken to the egg and shake to remove excess
  4. Add egged chicken to the flour and then to breadcrumbs
  5. Fry in a frying pan in 2cm of oil for 10 minutes or until cooked
  6. Cook 150 grams of sushi rice in 300ml of water for 10 minutes
  7. Leave rice to stand for 10 minutes before serving
