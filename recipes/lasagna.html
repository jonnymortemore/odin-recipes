Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the tastiest of all the food groups.

An Italian delicacy invented by the Romans to placate the god zeus.

Loved by cats and humans alike.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • 2 Onions
  • 10 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 Tinned Tomatoes
  • 2 tbs Sugar
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Dried Basil
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Make the ricotta mixture
  3. Layer the lasagna sheets with meat sauce and ricotta mixture in-between
  4. Cover with foil and bake for 60 minutes at 180c
  5. Enjoy!
